## Regulatory Landscape

The European Union's **AI Act** (Regulation EU 2024/1689) came into full force in August 2026 — the world's first comprehensive AI regulatory framework. Key requirements for generative AI (GPAI models):

- Disclosure that content is AI-generated (for synthetic media)
- Publication of training data summaries
- Compliance with EU copyright law for training data
- Systemic risk assessments for models above 10²⁵ FLOPs training compute

Large cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP) now capture approximately **38% of all generative AI API revenue** as the primary distribution channel for third-party models.
